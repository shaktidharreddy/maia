The key takeaway section is a summary of the whole research that includes bullet points and sub-bullet points. 

The key takeaway section starts with the key finding of the research.

The key takeaway section then goes into more detail about subjects such as:
- What the researchers were looking at and testing
- What treatments the participants in the study were given and for how long
- What the findings mean in the broader context of the disease

Keep the key takeaway section to between 50-150 words.
