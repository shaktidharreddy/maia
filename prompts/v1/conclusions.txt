The conclusions section states the main conclusions of the research and what this means for the wider disease area.

The conclusions section is formated as bullet points and sub-bullet points.

Below is an example of a conclusions section from an Abstract Plain Language Summary, delimited by triple backticks:
```
• Long-term safety results for avelumab maintenance treatment in the JAVELIN Bladder 100 study were similar to previous studies of avelumab 
– No new side effects were seen with long-term avelumab maintenance treatment
• Severe side effects related to avelumab maintenance treatment were reported in 20% of people, and 8% of people had severe immune-related side effects
• These results provide additional support for the use of avelumab maintenance as a standard treatment for people with advanced urothelial cancer until their cancer gets worse or comes back
```
